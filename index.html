Why PC is Better Than All Consoles

PC outperforms consoles in almost every single aspect, Having a keyboard supplies you with more key options for video games, having a mouse makes it so you can aim more accurately and swiftly.

In terms of Which one is better regarding graphics and processing speed, it isn't even a contest. Up until a while back consoles were stuck on 60 frames per second, while pc could have unlimited. Having more frames per second shows that the processing speed is faster and more capable it also allows for a smoother gaming experience. Pc players also have the option to many more games and a large variety of design and customization.

They can even have higher graphics which allows for a more beautiful game and enjoyable time. At the same time you can have multiple apps open and some PCs' being able to even run multiple games at once so you can maximize your enjoyment, such as having music or a video playing in the background.
